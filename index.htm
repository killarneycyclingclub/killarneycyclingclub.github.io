Killarney Cycling club is a dynamic, hardworking & community orientated cycling club that offers programs in Mountain Biking, Road & Leisure Cycling. We have a strong youth focus with a highly successful underage Mountain Bike program that has both a fun and competitive options 
We have also invested a lot of resources into Social Cycling with a hugely popular Leisure program for those who like to cycle in groups at their pace
